Features

Group Creation

Create your personalized trip to be able to participate in your favorite event. You set the place, the date of departure and arrival, the budget and choose whether to use the train or the car. Once finished, other people will be able to see your travel plan and decide whether to join you.
Otherwise, find a travel plan that satisfies you and join an existing group

Group Chat

To facilitate the organization of the group, use the dedicated chat without the need of using third parties applications

User Review System

Check out the reviews of a user and find the best fit of travel buddy in advance.
After completing a trip you can review your companions and describe their behaviors and habits. You can give feedback to everyone who travels after you so that you can know in advance if you are leaving with like-minded people
