TravelMate - Find your travel buddies

What is TravelMate?


A mobile application that connect students from 18 to 25 who want to go to an event in italy.

You can choose your event, find the best fit of travel mate in advance with low budget!!

HIGHLIGHTS

Target

Students

Our application is aimed at young people. Mostly university students between the ages of 18 and 25.

Events

It is very common for a university student to attend a concert, an art show, a technological event.

Low Budget

Traveling by train or car, in Italy, is the most convenient choice in terms of money spent.

FEATURES

What can you do with TravelMate

  • Formation Group
  • Review User System
  • Chat Communication

Create your travel plan

Or Join other one.

You can create your personalized trip to be able to participate in your favorite event. You can set the place, the date of departure and arrival, the budget and choose whether to use the train or the car. Once finished, other people will be able to see your travel plan and decide whether to join you

Otherwise, if you have already found a travel program that satisfies you, you can decide to join a trip organized by another person

Find the best fit of travel buddy in advance.

It's not easy to trust.

After completing a trip you can review your companions. You can describe their behaviors and habits, so you can give feedback to everyone who travels after you.

It will be easier to know in advance if you are leaving with like-minded people.

Chat System.

Communicating is easy.

To facilitate the organization it will be possible to use the chat. The travel groups will have a dedicated chat that will facilitate communication between users
